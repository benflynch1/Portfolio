Google x Freeletics

Launching a 14 day workout challenge, partnering with Freeletics and powered by voice through Google Assistant

01

The
Partnership

Combining Freeletics personal trainers into this Google Assistant action, ran workshops to define the type of fitness 'experience' that would best introduce new fitness enthusiasts into the world of Freeletics. A 14 day fitness challenge was born that would gradually increase participants workout schedule in a safe and controlled way. Cumulating in a final 'challenge' on the 14th day.

02

The
Workout

Freeletics instructor Selene was selected as the 'voice' of the brand to motivate users through the 14-day challenge. Knowing that our target audience was primarily new fitness enthusiasts, our hypothesis was that equipment-free workouts would best help users navigate a voice-activated workout experience. We utilised Freeletics trainers experience to select workout types and for how long each section should be.

03

Enhancing the
conversational design

Alongside each workout, a GIF would accompany the instructions and guide the user throughout the movements of each exercise. Through extensive UXR, we uncovered that participants found the GIFs and interactions slightly too interruptive. Through rounds of iterations we reduced the average number of 'actions' by 33%.

I was also tasked to create visuals that best accompany the experience at the lowest file size feasible and in the most engaging format with the assets provided. We had to partner with Google Assistant's product team to overcome technical limitations around file sizes throughout the process.

04

Encouraging
rest days

To keep users engaged in the platform on non-workout days, the Assistant Action provides recommendations around nutrition, customer success stories, motivational Spotify playlists and more. Further static imagery was created to signpost key moments for the user during their plan.

Content ranged from recipe development, to inspirational customer stories to motivational playlists using Freeletics brand identity.
