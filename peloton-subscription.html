01

About
the project

Following Peloton's strategic shift to an app-first subscription model, all B2B partners globally needed to be migrated onto the new tiered pricing structure. This project ensured the transition was commercially sound and user-experience coherent for operators across hospitality, corporate wellness, and multi-site fitness.

02

The
Challenge

B2B clients had diverse contract types, user volumes, and access needs. Designing a flexible tier system that met commercial targets while remaining comprehensible to both operators and their end users — across multiple regions and languages — was a complex systems design challenge.

03

Design
Outcomes

A scalable tier framework was designed, tested with key B2B accounts, and rolled out globally. Clear operator-facing documentation and end-user UI were developed to support adoption. The project met its commercial rollout targets and reduced inbound support queries from confused operators.
