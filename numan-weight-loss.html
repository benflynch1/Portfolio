01

About
the project

Numan is a men's health platform offering clinically-backed treatments. The weight management programme pairs GLP-1 medication with coaching, delivered via a mobile app. The project focused on understanding where users were dropping off and redesigning the experience to improve programme retention.

02

The
Challenge

Retention in long-term health programmes is notoriously difficult. Users on a multi-month treatment journey need to feel consistently supported, motivated, and informed. The coach-led app had to deliver that at scale — without the cost of 1-to-1 human coaching for every interaction.

03

Design
Outcomes

Through data analysis, user interviews, and rapid experimentation, we identified and addressed the key drop-off points — check-in fatigue, unclear progress signalling, and weak coach communication patterns. A/B tested improvements drove measurable gains in 30 and 60-day retention rates.
