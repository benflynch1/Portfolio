All work

Designing for the Nike+ ecosystem — a personalised feed, a connected story across Nike's apps, and exclusive product drops reserved for members.

Client
Nike (Portland)
Sector
Sport · Consumer
Tags
Mobile App Personalisation Ecosystem Membership
Role
Product Designer, AKQA London
Brand challenge

Give Nike+ members a single home for product drops, athlete content and exclusive access — without fragmenting the wider app ecosystem.

Approach

A personalised feed pattern, an ecosystem map across Nike.com, Training, Running and SNKRS, and an inbox-led "Reserved for You" flow.

Outcome

A dynamic feed and inbox experience that shipped to iOS and Android, picked up coverage from Forbes, Fortune and Complex.

01

A personal
news feed

The Nike+ App opens onto a personalised feed: cards surface new sneaker drops, social activity from Nike+ friends, and campaigns tailored to each member. I supported the launch by designing UI treatments for the feed card, including content patterns for product display.

The challenge was balancing density with energy — keeping the visual language unmistakably Nike, while letting the content do the talking and routing members efficiently into product, services and events.

02

Connecting the
product ecosystem

Nike's digital footprint spans the .com store, Training, Running and SNKRS. I worked with the core Nike team to visualise how those products connect, mapping dependencies and desired states across Inbox, Feed and Store features.

The work made the trade-offs visible: where to consolidate, where to specialise, and how a member's journey could feel continuous across surfaces that had grown up independently.

03

Reserved
for you

Exclusive products and drops are surfaced to members as "Reserved for You" — a dedicated flow that runs from a notification or inbox card straight through to checkout.

I led design exploration across the flow, from notification framing to wireframes and final UI treatments. The pattern needed to feel premium but unfussy: clear what was being offered, clear how long the member had, and a frictionless route to purchase.
